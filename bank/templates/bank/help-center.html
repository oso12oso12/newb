Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transfer money between my accounts?
Go to the Transfer page from the sidebar, choose your source and destination accounts, enter an amount, and confirm. Transfers between your own accounts post instantly.
How long do deposits take to clear?
Most mobile check deposits and transfers clear within one business day. Larger deposits may be held for up to two business days for verification.
How do I report a lost or stolen card?
Visit Accounts > Manage Card to freeze it instantly, or call us at 1-800-555-0182 any time to report it lost or stolen and request a replacement.
Where can I update my contact information?
Head to Settings to update your email, phone number, and mailing address, or to change your notification preferences.
